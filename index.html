Privacy Policy

NeonDrive X - Endless 3D Racing

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Santosh Mishra ("we", "our", or "us") operates the NeonDrive X mobile application (the "Service"). This Service is provided by Santosh Mishra at no cost and is intended for use as is.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy details how your information is handled, especially considering our game operates mostly offline but utilizes third-party advertising services.

1. Architecture & Data Storage (Offline First)

Your Game Data is Yours. NeonDrive X is designed to be fully playable offline. All your game progress is stored locally on your device.

Local Game Data

We store the following data locally on your device's internal memory to save your progress:

  • Game Progress: Total Coins, High Scores, and Games Played.
  • Purchases: Unlocked Car Skins (e.g., Classic Blue, City Taxi, Golden King).
  • Settings: Sound Volume, Graphics Quality, and Difficulty.

⚠️ Data Retention: We do not upload your gameplay data to any external server. If you clear the app's data or uninstall the game, your progress, coins, and unlocked skins will be permanently lost.

2. Information Collection and Third-Party Services

We DO NOT collect personal information like your name, email, or phone number. However, when connected to the internet, the app uses third-party services that may collect specific identifiers.

Device Information & Permissions

  • Internet Access: Required ONLY to fetch and display Google AdMob advertisements.
  • Vibrate: Used for in-game haptic feedback (collisions).

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep NeonDrive X free, we use Google AdMob. When you are online, AdMob may collect data to provide relevant ads and analyze ad performance.

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID (AAID): A unique, resettable ID for personalized advertising.
  • Device Data: OS version, device model, and general location (IP address).

Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized ads in your Android Settings: Settings > Privacy > Ads > Delete advertising ID.

For more details, please review the Google AdMob Privacy Policy.

4. Data Deletion Request (Play Store Compliance)

Because we do not use user accounts or cloud servers, there is no account for us to delete on our end. You have complete control over your data.

To completely delete all your game data (coins, scores) and advertising cache:

  1. Go to your device Settings > Apps > NeonDrive X.
  2. Tap on Storage & cache.
  3. Tap Clear Data (or simply Uninstall the game).

5. Children’s Privacy

NeonDrive X is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Furthermore, we configure our AdMob settings to restrict personalized ads for users identified as children, complying with Google Play's Families Policy.

6. Security

All local game data is sandboxed securely by the Android OS. Ad network data transmission is secured via HTTPS.
