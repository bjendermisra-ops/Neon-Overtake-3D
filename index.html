Santosh Mishra ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Neon Overtake 3D: Car Racing mobile application (the "Service"). This Service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

1. Data Storage (Offline Gameplay)

Your data stays on your device.

This game is designed to work offline. We do NOT store your data on any server.

We store the following locally:

  • Game progress (coins, scores)
  • Unlocked items
  • Settings (sound, graphics)
⚠️ If you uninstall or clear app data, all progress will be permanently deleted.

We do NOT collect personal data such as name, email, or phone number.

However, third-party services may collect limited technical data.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may use cookies, advertising IDs, and similar technologies to provide and personalize ads.

AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Device information
  • IP address (approximate location)

Users can opt out of personalized ads:
Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete Advertising ID

More info: Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Deletion

We do not store user data on servers.

To delete all data:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Neon Overtake 3D
  3. Tap Storage
  4. Click Clear Data or Uninstall

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

AdMob settings are configured to comply with Google Play Families Policy.

6. Security

All data is stored securely within your device. Ad data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.
