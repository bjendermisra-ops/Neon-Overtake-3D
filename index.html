Neon Overtake 3D: Car Racing

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Santosh Mishra ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Neon Overtake 3D: Car Racing mobile application (the "Service"). This Service is provided at no cost and is intended for use "as is". This document informs visitors and players regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information for those who use our Service.

1. Data Storage & Online Connectivity

Online Gameplay & Cloud Synchronization:

Neon Overtake 3D: Car Racing requires an active internet connection to deliver its core features, including dynamic advertisements and global services. While the game operates online, we do NOT collect or store your personal identification data on our personal servers.

The following game-related data is processed and stored locally on your device (and may be synced via official Google Play Services):

  • Game progress (coins, high scores, levels completed)
  • Unlocked items and vehicles
  • User preferences and settings (sound, graphics quality)
⚠️ Important Note: If you uninstall the app or clear the app's cache/data from your device settings without a cloud backup via Google Play Games, your local game progress will be permanently deleted.

2. Information Collection

We deeply respect your privacy. We do NOT actively collect personal data such as your real name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

However, because this is an online connected game, certain third-party services integrated into our app (such as Google Play Services and Advertising networks) may automatically collect limited technical data to ensure the game functions properly and to serve relevant content.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep the game free for everyone, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Ad networks require internet access to serve ads and measure their performance.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may use cookies, advertising IDs, and similar tracking technologies to provide, personalize, and optimize ad delivery based on your interests.

AdMob may automatically collect the following technical data:

  • Advertising ID (AAID): A unique, user-resettable ID for advertising.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and network type.
  • IP Address: Used strictly for approximate geographical location (country/city level) to serve relevant regional ads.
  • App Usage Data: Interactions with the ads displayed within the game.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads:
You have full control over your advertising preferences. You can opt out of personalized advertisements at any time through your Android device settings:

Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete Advertising ID (or "Opt out of Ads Personalization")

For more comprehensive information on how Google uses this data, please review the: Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Deletion Policy

Under strict data protection guidelines (including Google Play's Data Safety policies), users must have a clear path to delete their data. Since we do not transmit or store your personal identity data on any external private database, there is no personal profile on our end to delete.

To completely erase all your game data and ad-tracking history from this app, follow these steps:

  1. Open your device Settings.
  2. Navigate to Apps or App Management.
  3. Locate and select Neon Overtake 3D.
  4. Tap on Storage & Cache.
  5. Click Clear Data and Clear Cache, or simply Uninstall the game.
  6. To reset ad tracking, reset your Advertising ID in your device's Privacy settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

This application is designed to be safe and suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly prompt or collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

Furthermore, our Google AdMob settings are specifically configured to comply strictly with the Google Play Families Policy and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information (which is technically not supported by our app), please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

6. Security

We value your trust in playing our game. All local data is stored within the secure, sandboxed environment of your device's operating system. Any technical data transmitted to third-party services (like AdMob or Google Play Services) is securely encrypted and transmitted over HTTPS/TLS secure protocols. While no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, we use commercially acceptable means to protect your experience.
